Area

"It's a beach town, always has been".

Located on the west coat shore, we have a cute little beach town called Blackpool. This is a very tourist heavy place with many places to do and see. There's roughly 140,000 people living in Blackpool, not even including all of the tourists! Their economy relies on tourism, as nearly 20 million people visited last year, claimed our interviewee. And rightfully so, there's a lot of attractions which are all upkept extremely well. Additionally, the locals there are very welcoming and are more than happy to assist you if you are in any need of help. With that being said, you will find a variety of landscapes here such as sandy beaches, grassy fields, and lots of clay hills.

Climate

"You just have to like the rain, it never stops".

It was raining every step of the way throughout the visit here in Blackpool, but one can not help but appreciate the gorgeous reflections on the wood floors. It does have it's fair share of bright days, but it still gets very cold. It averages around 30-40F, so make sure to put on a raincoat if you go outside. This does not stop the locals from wearing their favorite shorts or skirts, though!
