Welcome to Blackpool!

"What's a Californian doing here?"

I had the chance to visit the fantastic town of Blackpool! Located along the west coast of the United Kingdom, Blackpool sits there as a great tourist destination for all with it's many attractions. I also had the pleasure of interviewing own of their locals, who has resided in this comfy town for roughly 20 years. They described Blackpool's many features and sites to see, as well as many interesting facts! They made sure to cover a wide range of topics such as their location, local history, attractions, and even art exhibits. They were also very eager to showcase their local Facebook page that has many pictures to share, and I am happily showcasing some of them within this site. If you're thinking about visiting Blackpool, or even just want to know more about it from a local's perspective, explore this website.
