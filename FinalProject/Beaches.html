A Whole Bunch!

"So many to 'sea', get it?"

After walking the local beach for some time, I had assumed there was one or maybe even two beaches. However, there are a total of 23 beaches here in Blackpool!. Though, according to our interviewee, the most popular beaches by far are Blackpool North, Bispham, and Cleveleys. You may not find people playing in the water during the year, as it is freezing, however you will commonly see people walking by the shore with their friends or their pets. Additionally, the beaches here vary in the quality of sand. Some beaches have very fine sand, whereas others will be composed of rock. Regardless, beware of the changing tides as this commonly takes place every other day!
