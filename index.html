Welcome

Looking for a place to unwind and relax? Want to get away from your everyday troubles? Do you have a desire for outdoor activities but just can’t find the time? Activities

Why should royalty like yourself just sit in your room? Just a stone throw away there are so many activities to participate in: canoeing, skiing, spa's and so much more! However, if you find yourself tuckered out from a day of fun, have no fear! Our lodge has several sitting rooms, each with their own television for all of your viewing needs. If you feel the need to share this experience with your friends, you absolutely can! Our lodge offers free WiFi for all guests.
