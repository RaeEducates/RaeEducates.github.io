Welcome

Looking for a place to unwind and relax? Want to get away from your everyday troubles? Do you have a desire for outdoor activities but just can’t find the time? Then you’ve just so happen to stumble onto the right website! Here at Home Away From Home we offer luxurious rooms in a large, multi story cabin with a grand lake view just outside our front doors. Each day, we serve spectacular meals, cooked to perfection for our perfect guests. There is no limit to how much you can eat, so please, eat as much as you'd like! Additionally, our guests get special offers at our nearby town, which is full of many activities at a discounted price. With the numerous kinds of rooms, we are sure that you will able to make yourself home...away from home!


Activities

Why should royalty like yourself just sit in your room? Just a stone throw away there are so many activities to participate in: canoeing, skiing, spa's and so much more! However, if you find yourself tuckered out from a day of fun, have no fear! Our lodge has several sitting rooms, each with their own television for all of your viewing needs. Additionally, each night we offer special treats to all of those that sit around the campfire just outside of the cabin. If you feel the need to share this experience with your friends, you absolutely can! Our lodge offers free WiFi for all guests.
